Projects

Assignment Tracker

Assignment Tracker is a full-stack application that keeps track of assignments. I created this app for a friend who coaches students to get into college and wanted a way for her clients to keep track of their tasks. With Assignment Tracker, you can document assignments by creating, updating, and deleting assignments. Once an assignment is completed you can celebrate by clicking the complete button for a confetti rain! Technologies Used: JavaScript, jQuery, Handlebars.js, Confetti.js, Bootstrap, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL.


Rate My Hike

Rate My Hike is a full-stack application that helps users keep track of places they have hiked. An avid hiker myself, I wanted a place to record my hikes for future refernce. With Rate My Hike, you can keep track of hikes you have taken by creating, updating, and deleting hikes. Technologies Used: JavaScript, jQuery, Handlebars.js, Bootstrap, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL.


Nozama - Inventory Management System

Nozama - Inventory Management System is a full-stack application that helps managers keep track of items on hand. With Nozama, you can keep track of items by creating, updating, and deleting items. Technologies Used: JavaScript, jQuery, Handlebars.js, Bootstrap, Express, and MongoDB.


Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe was my first front-end application to play an online game of Tic Tac Toe. Technologies Used: JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS.
